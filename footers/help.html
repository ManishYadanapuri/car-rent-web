Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car? +
You need a valid driver's license (at least 1 year old), a government-issued ID (Aadhaar/Passport), and a credit/debit card for the security deposit. International customers need an International Driving Permit along with their home country license.
What is the minimum age to rent a car? +
The minimum age is 21 years for standard vehicles. For luxury and sports cars, the minimum age is 25 years. All drivers must have held a valid license for at least 1 year.
Is fuel included in the rental price? +
Cars are provided with a full tank of fuel. You must return the vehicle with a full tank, otherwise refueling charges will apply. For electric vehicles, we provide charging recommendations and access to our partner charging stations.
Can I add an additional driver? +
Yes, you can add additional drivers at the time of booking or pickup. Each additional driver must meet our age and license requirements. The fee is ₹500 per additional driver per day.
What happens if I return the car late? +
We offer a 30-minute grace period. After that, late fees apply: up to 2 hours late = 50% of daily rate; more than 2 hours = full additional day charge. Please contact us if you anticipate being late.
What is included in the insurance? +
Basic insurance covers third-party liability and theft protection. Our Premium Insurance (optional) includes zero excess, personal accident cover, roadside assistance, and windscreen/tire damage. Visit our Insurance page for full details.
