Cancellation Policy

Flexible cancellation options for your peace of mind

✅

More than 48 hours before pickup

Cancel your booking more than 48 hours before the scheduled pickup time and receive a complete refund. No questions asked.

100% Refund
⚠️

24 to 48 hours before pickup

Cancellations made between 24-48 hours before pickup will receive a partial refund. A small fee is retained for reservation costs.

75% Refund
🕐

Less than 24 hours before pickup

Last-minute cancellations within 24 hours of pickup time will receive 50% refund. The remaining covers operational costs.

50% Refund
❌

No-show or after pickup time

If you don't show up or cancel after the scheduled pickup time, unfortunately no refund can be provided.

No Refund

How to Cancel Your Booking

1

Login

Sign in to your LuxRide account on our website or app

2

My Bookings

Go to "My Bookings" and select the reservation to cancel

3

Cancel

Click "Cancel Booking" and confirm your cancellation

4

Refund

Refund processed within 5-7 business days to original payment method

📋 Important Notes

  • All times are calculated based on the scheduled pickup time in local timezone.
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.
  • For bookings made with promotional codes or discounts, the discount value may be non-refundable.
  • Premium and supercar bookings may have different cancellation terms – check your booking confirmation.
  • In case of emergencies or exceptional circumstances, please contact our support team for assistance.
  • Modifications to booking dates/times may be available as an alternative to cancellation.
