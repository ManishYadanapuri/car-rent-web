2. Eligibility Requirements

To rent a vehicle from LuxRide, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Be at least 21 years of age (25 years for luxury and sports cars)
  • Hold a valid driver's license for at least 1 year
  • Provide a valid government-issued ID (Aadhaar, Passport, or Voter ID)
  • Have a valid credit or debit card for security deposit
  • Not have any major traffic violations in the past 3 years

Note: International customers must present an International Driving Permit (IDP) along with their home country license.

3. Booking & Reservations

Reservations can be made through our website, mobile app, or by contacting our customer service. All bookings are subject to vehicle availability.

3.1 Booking Confirmation

A booking is confirmed only after you receive a confirmation email with your booking ID. Unconfirmed bookings do not guarantee vehicle availability.

3.2 Modification of Bookings

You may modify your booking (dates, vehicle, location) subject to availability and any applicable price differences. Modifications within 24 hours of pickup may incur additional charges.

4. Payment Terms

Payment is required at the time of booking. We accept credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and net banking.

4.1 Security Deposit

A refundable security deposit is required and will be blocked on your card at pickup. The deposit amount varies by vehicle category:

  • Standard vehicles: ₹5,000 - ₹10,000
  • Premium vehicles: ₹15,000 - ₹25,000
  • Luxury & Sports cars: ₹50,000 - ₹2,00,000

4.2 Additional Charges

Additional charges may apply for: late returns, fuel difference, traffic violations, tolls, cleaning fees, and damage repairs.

5. Rental Agreement

Upon pickup, you will sign a rental agreement that includes vehicle condition, fuel level, and mileage. Please inspect the vehicle carefully before accepting.

The rental period begins at the scheduled pickup time and ends at the scheduled return time. Early pickups or late returns may result in additional charges.

6. Vehicle Use & Restrictions

The rented vehicle may only be operated by the authorized driver(s) listed in the rental agreement. The following activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs
  • Using the vehicle for racing, off-road driving (unless rented for that purpose), or illegal activities
  • Smoking inside the vehicle
  • Transporting hazardous materials
  • Sub-renting or lending the vehicle to unauthorized persons
  • Driving outside permitted geographical areas without prior approval
