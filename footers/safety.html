Safe Driving Guidelines

1

Familiarize Yourself with the Vehicle

Before starting your journey, take time to understand the car's controls, mirrors, and safety features.

2

Always Wear Your Seatbelt

Ensure all passengers are buckled up before moving. It's the law and your first line of protection.

3

Follow Speed Limits

Adhere to posted speed limits and adjust speed according to weather and road conditions.

4

No Drunk Driving

Never operate a vehicle under the influence of alcohol or drugs. Zero tolerance policy applies.

5

Avoid Distractions

Keep your phone away while driving. Use hands-free devices for navigation and calls.

6

Take Breaks on Long Drives

Stop every 2-3 hours to rest. Fatigue is a major cause of accidents on highways.
