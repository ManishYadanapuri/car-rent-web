Our Story

Founded in 2015, LuxRide began with a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize the car rental experience in India. What started as a small fleet of 10 luxury vehicles in Mumbai has grown into the nation's premier premium car rental service.

Our founder, Arjun Verma, noticed a gap in the market – people wanted access to premium vehicles without the hassle of ownership. From business executives needing a Mercedes for client meetings to couples dreaming of a Ferrari for their wedding, everyone deserved a seamless experience.

Today, we operate across 50+ cities with a fleet of 500+ vehicles, ranging from elegant sedans to exotic supercars. But our mission remains the same: making every journey memorable.

Our Journey

2015 - The Beginning

LuxRide founded in Mumbai with 10 luxury vehicles and a dream to transform car rentals.

2017 - Expansion

Expanded to Delhi, Bangalore, and Pune. Fleet grew to 100 vehicles including supercars.

2019 - Tech Innovation

Launched our mobile app with instant booking. Introduced GPS tracking in all vehicles.

2021 - Going Green

Added 50+ electric and hybrid vehicles. Committed to sustainable luxury.

2023 - 50K Milestone

Served our 50,000th customer. Expanded to 50+ cities across India.

2024 - Today

500+ vehicles, 24/7 support, and India's highest-rated premium car rental service.
