Insurance FAQ

What is an excess/deductible? +
The excess (or deductible) is the amount you pay out of pocket before insurance kicks in. For example, if you have a ₹10,000 excess and damage costs ₹25,000, you pay ₹10,000 and insurance covers ₹15,000. With Elite plan, you have zero excess – we cover everything.
What happens if I have an accident? +
1. Ensure everyone's safety and call emergency services if needed. 2. Call our 24/7 helpline immediately. 3. Take photos of the damage and accident scene. 4. Get details of other parties involved. 5. File a police report if required. 6. Our team will guide you through the claim process.
What is NOT covered by insurance? +
Insurance does not cover: damage caused while driving under the influence of alcohol/drugs, damage from racing or reckless driving, damage to interiors due to smoking, loss of personal belongings (except Elite plan), damage caused by unauthorized drivers, or violations of rental agreement terms.
Can I use my own car insurance? +
Personal car insurance typically does not cover rental vehicles. Some premium credit cards offer rental car insurance – check with your card provider. We recommend our insurance plans for comprehensive protection.
How do I upgrade my insurance? +
You can upgrade your insurance: 1. During the booking process on our website/app. 2. At the pickup location before starting your rental. 3. Contact customer support to upgrade mid-rental (prorated charges apply).
